Questions

What the free audit actually checks.

What does the free audit check?

The audit inspects your public homepage plus root discovery files: robots.txt, llms.txt, and sitemap.xml. It scores crawler access, entity and content evidence, and answer-engine crawl policy, then names a repair for every failed signal.

Does a high score mean AI engines will cite my site?

No. A high score is not proof of an AI citation. Buyer-prompt evidence requires separate, timestamped runs inside each answer engine, which this audit does not run.

How many free audits can I run?

One free audit per browser, on a public website, with no signup required.

What happens after I get my results?

The report shows a weighted readiness score, a prioritized repair list, and a full findings ledger. Suede Scan can package the repairs as implementation-ready fixes or ship the edits with before-and-after evidence.
