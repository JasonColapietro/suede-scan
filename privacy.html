Privacy

What happens when you run a free audit.

This page explains what Suede Audit does with the domain you submit at audit.suedeai.ai.

What the audit fetches

Suede Audit fetches the public homepage, robots.txt, llms.txt, and sitemap.xml of the domain you submit. It does not log in, does not access private or authenticated pages, and rejects private IP addresses, internal hostnames, and non-public destinations as a target.

What is logged

Each completed audit logs the submitted domain, the resulting score, and how long the check took, to server logs used for monitoring and debugging. Failed audits log the domain and an error message. These are operational records for keeping the service running, not a public or shared database.

Abuse prevention

A short-lived, in-memory counter limits how many audits a single IP address can run per minute. This counter is not written to disk and resets automatically.

Your browser

A cookie named suede_audit_used records that this browser has already used its one free audit; it expires after one year. Your finished report is cached in your browser's local storage so you can reopen it without running another scan. Suede Audit does not require an account, an email address, or a signup to run the free audit.

Third parties

Suede Audit does not sell submitted domains or audit results to third parties.

Contact

Questions about this page, or a request about logged data: info@suedeai.org.
